BLOCKCHAIN

Leading Blockchain Technology Expertise in Korea

Hillstone Partners recognized the innovative potential of blockchain technology early on and strategically entered the virtual asset market by establishing the 'Hillstone Blockchain Research Institute' for systematic research and development. Based on our extensive M&A and investment experience, Hillstone Partners has continuously pursued investments and mergers & acquisitions by rigorously selecting blockchain companies according to strict criteria.

Currently, Hillstone Partners is focused on serving as a critical bridge connecting traditional financial systems with decentralized finance (DeFi) ecosystems, providing investors with a new dimension of financial services. We are building an innovative platform that utilizes blockchain networks, cryptocurrencies, NFTs, and metaverse infrastructure to enable investors to easily conclude investment contracts and safely transfer assets within the scope permitted by current legal frameworks.

Hillstone Partners' ultimate goal is to build strong connections with various services available in decentralized finance, ultimately growing into a comprehensive virtual asset financial platform that is completely decentralized.

Hillstone Finance

Hillstone Partners' core objective is to enable all individual investors to easily access high-yield investment products that were previously difficult to access. Traditionally, highly profitable funds including private equity funds have been operated in structures that are very difficult for individual investors to access.

Hillstone Partners aims to remove the geographical and economic barriers of these closed financial markets through innovative blockchain technology. We transparently provide detailed information about various alternative investment products that have been difficult for individual investors to access, including private equity funds, global fund products, crypto fund products, and digital asset management, supporting investors to make investment decisions more easily and quickly.

Transparent Financial DAO Implementation

Hillstone Partners has made establishing a completely transparently operated Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO) a core strategy. The DAO that Hillstone Partners is building overcomes the limitations of existing centralized organizational structures and presents an innovative governance model where all stakeholders can participate in fair and transparent decision-making processes.

Transparent Decision Making

All work and decision-making processes are transparently recorded on the blockchain, allowing participants to monitor and participate in the decision-making process in real-time.

Professional Standing Committee

The standing committee consists of specialized institutions in blockchain and finance fields, rigorously sourcing and verifying various investment products to be uploaded to the DAO platform using virtual assets.

Decentralization

All processes proceed in a completely decentralized manner without intermediaries, ensuring efficiency and reliability through automated execution via smart contracts.

Complete Transparency

All transaction records, decision-making processes, and fund flows are permanently recorded on the blockchain, providing complete transparency that anyone can verify and confirm.

RWA NFT Issuance

Hillstone Partners aims to achieve innovative realization of small-scale investment, transparent trading, and fractional ownership by converting various real-world assets such as real estate, artworks, and bonds into blockchain-based NFTs through the RWA (Real World Asset) approach.

Hillstone Partners' RWA-based NFTs are innovative technology that securely proves fractional ownership of physical assets in digital NFT form, enabling anyone to easily invest in and trade high-value assets that previously required large capital.

Real Estate Investment

Fractionalizing commercial buildings and residential real estate into NFTs to provide opportunities for small investors to invest in premium real estate.

Art Investment

Tokenizing high-value artworks and rare collections to lower the barriers to art investment and provide liquidity.

Bond Products

Implementing various bond products such as corporate bonds and government bonds as NFTs to build a more efficient bond trading market.
